Appoint someone to deal with HMRC for you

Use this service to allow someone else (they'll be referred to as your 'agent') to talk to HMRC about your:

  • Self Assessment tax returns
  • National Insurance contributions
  • Tax Credit claims

Who you can appoint

You can appoint an accountant or tax adviser, a friend or relative or someone from a voluntary organisation.

Your agent must be registered for HMRC online services. You'll need to know their Government Gateway Agent ID.

If you don't know the person's Agent ID, you can complete and send a PDF form instead.
