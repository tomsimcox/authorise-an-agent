Appoint someone to handle your tax return

Use this service to allow someone else to deal with your Self Assessment tax return. This person will be known as your agent. An agent can be:

  • an accountant or tax adviser
  • a friend or relative
  • someone from a voluntary organisation

You can also allow them to deal with HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) about other matters. These include National Insurance contributions and current Tax Credit claims.

Your agent must be registered for HMRC online services. You'll need to know their Government Gateway Agent ID.

If you don't know the person's Agent ID, you can complete and send a PDF form instead.
